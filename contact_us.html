ABOUT US


It's been a long journey for Uttara Foods to be what it is today. It goes back to the year 1991, when the company was founded by 3 Directors namely Dr. Girish Kolwankar, Mr. Neil Dalgado and Dr Shyam Dhawan. The Company started as a Feed Manufacturing firm with a first year turnover of Rs. 1.2 crores. It diversified with forward integration into contract broiler farming.. It has since grown to have its operations in Maharashtra.

Uttara Foods enjoys reputation of brand image in the poultry feed market for its good and consistent feed quality. It has loyal customers who wouldn't switch to competing brands. Uttara Foods , was started during the year 1995, with the setting up of a breeding farm next to Kamshet, Dist Pune, Maharashtra and the hatchery in Kolghar, near Poynad. With the setting up of these facilities, the company started selling Day Old Broiler Chicks to poultry farmers.


Integrated Poultry farming was started by the company, during the year 2000, with branches across the state of Maharashtra.

The Company is also in the business of Feed Sales to Individual Open farmers. The company maintains its reputation for its quality in the feed it sells. Some of the key products are Poultry Pellet Feed for Broiler birds, (Starter and Finisher). Broiler 50% Concentrate and Broiler 10% Concentrate.To meet its growing requirement of hatching eggs, the company started its own breeding farm at Lonand Maharashtra.
